How it works

Are you hungry? So are we. But with the busy lifestyles we live today, its hard to find time and energy to whip up a good bite to eat. Sure, there’s fast food, but the quality is mediocre, its unhealthy, and it can take a while. On the other hand, you have barely any time to cook while juggling all the things you do — whether you are a student, an employee, or a parent. Divvy A Meal is here to make your life easy, so you never again have to worry about what to make for dinner- or worse, have Ramen.

Dietary restrictions? We have you covered. On a tight budget? Pick a price range that works for you. Feel like having something exotic? Choose from a selection of cuisines. Once you find a meal you like, click purchase, and you’ll receive an email confirmation with information on where to go and how you can contact the lister. It’s that easy. So go back to be awesome. We’ll take care of the food.

Maybe you don’t fall into the above category — you actually can find time to cook or eat out. But you too have a problem — leftovers. We’re not talking about your last bite of casserole — you can keep that. But if you have a portion or more of leftover food, you can divvy it with someone, making their day, and yours, a whole lot simpler.

Just login and click Divvy A Meal, and share your great food with others. Once someone purchases your listing, you receive their contact information and they’ll come and get it. It’s that easy.
